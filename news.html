Why are so many young Hongkongers suddenly joining working holiday?

More young Hongkongers dream of swapping at least a few months for more fulfilling work overseas and it seems that the interest has been on the rise.



According to the statistics from Labour Department, since 2001, when Hong Kong government has established working holiday scheme(WHS), there have been more than 78,000 people aged from 18 to 30 participating in the scheme.

Melissa is a final-year International Journalism student of Hong Kong Baptist University. She is now in her 20s and in love with travelling. After going to Sweden as an exchange student in 2016, she suddenly realised the importance of a work-life balance which is something Hongkongers have been struggling with for years. This is how she came up with the idea of honours project: Working Holiday.
