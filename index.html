Are you eligible to join?

Age range

Be aged between 18 and 30 at the time of making application

Passport

Be ordinarily resident in the HKSAR and hold a valid HKSAR or British National (Overseas) passport

Financial proof

Hold a return travel ticket or provide a proof of financial resources to purchase such ticket and support your expenses in the host economy

Insurance

Take out insurance plans throughout your stay in the host country, except Australia

This is how she came up with the idea of honours project: Working Holiday.
